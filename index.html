Medieval Rush

INSTRUCTIONS

Blue player

WASD keys to move

Space bar to change unit being controlled

-----

Green units can move, then they must rest

Yellow Player

IJKL keys to move

Left arrow key to change unit being controlled

-----

Units regain health when not moving

Red player

Arrow keys to move

Numpad enter to change unit being controlled

-----

Bases spawn new units every 20 seconds
